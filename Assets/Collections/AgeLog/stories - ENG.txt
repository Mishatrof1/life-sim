Brief for the scriptwriter This is an idle life simulator about the periods of life. There is the button "+age", clicking on which you will add one year to the character's age. Each click trigger an event connected with the age. At the moment of "growing-up" (after clicking the button) players will get pop up notification with the event description. The player must choose between 3 variants of actions for each event. Each action leads to definite consequence. Consequences are divided into 4 groups, specifying the life sphere which can be effected by the action: relationship, health, happiness and appearence (attraction).   Target audience: teenage players, 16+. The story should be engaging to the target audience.  Narration genre: an absorbing, challenging story about the life periods. Must contain some comic (trash) situations understandable for teenagers. Humor should be dirty, nasty etc. The narration must contain love stories, parties, sex, pregnance, abortions and so on.													33	6	198
